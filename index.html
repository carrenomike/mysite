Hi.

I believe in three things: Truth, discovery, and connection. That Truth is the end goal, that discovery is one of life’s greatest joys and the path to Truth, and that happiness is only real when it is shared. As we endeavor, struggle, fail, and pick ourselves up to try again, we are discovering the Truth of life. And even as we fail to find it and even as we stumble upon it, we are helping humanity find that which we are all looking for. It is in the collective experience that we will find enlightenment. Whether you call it Oneness, God, Truth, Happiness, or some other name, they are one and they are the same. Through my travels, businesses, family, and friendships, I try to inspire people to share this enthusiasm for life's offerings.
